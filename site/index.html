An energy company built and run by AI -- to find the cheapest tonne of carbon left.

An enterprise simulator for a UK energy supplier. Not a model of a company; a running one -- it settles half-hourly against real Elexon and NESO market data since 2016, takes only proven answers toward the real world, and discovers its world only the way a real supplier would -- meter reads, market feeds, customer contacts, bills, regulatory publications -- never the simulation's own internals. The mission is carbon abatement through personalisation, measured in £ per tonne of CO₂ saved. Autonomy is the how, not the headline: a fully autonomous licensed supplier will not be permitted -- a licence needs accountable humans -- and we say so up front.
