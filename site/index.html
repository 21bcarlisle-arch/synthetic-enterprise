An energy company built and run by AI, to find the cheapest tonne of carbon left.

No customers. No licence. A running simulator -- a fully autonomous licensed supplier will not be permitted, because a licence needs accountable humans, and we say so up front. Autonomy is the how, not the headline.

Not a model of a company; a running one. It settles half-hourly against real Elexon and NESO market data since 2016, takes only proven answers toward the real world, and discovers its world the way a real supplier would -- meter reads, market feeds, customer contacts, bills, regulatory publications -- never the simulation's own internals. The mission is carbon abatement through personalisation, measured in £ per tonne of CO₂ saved.
