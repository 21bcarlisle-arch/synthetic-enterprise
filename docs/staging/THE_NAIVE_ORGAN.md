# THE NAIVE ORGAN — manufacture the outside view (P1, QUEUE, rank high)

**Staged:** 2026-07-13 by advisor. **Director-decided.** The advisor claimed the
naive outside question was the irreplaceable human contribution. The director
corrected him: *"That doesn't require a human, far from it. This can be part of
the process."* He is right, and the correction is important enough to build.

## The insight
The director's own COLD_EYES insight already contains this: **vantage, not
capability.** COLD_EYES applies a blindfolded fresh instance to ARTEFACTS (a
page, a number). The naive question applies the same protocol to the SYSTEM —
its claims, its state, its stuck-ness. Same principle, bigger target.

**Every structural finding of the last three days came from a naive question
the machine and the advisor could not ask, because both were reasoning INSIDE
the system's frame.** That frame was the thing that was wrong.

## The triggers are mechanisable (each one is a real catch from this weekend)
Fire the naive organ on any of these observable conditions:
1. **Flat metric while the system reports healthy** — "why hasn't X moved?"
   (caught: three hours of idle turns)
2. **A TERMINAL-STATE claim** — "exhausted", "nothing to do", "complete", "no
   candidates", "blocked". Naive organ MUST independently check the raw data.
   (caught: "the map is exhausted" with 31 open atoms)
3. **An INHERENCE claim** — "physics", "inherent", "unavoidable", "by design",
   "cannot be parallelised". Naive organ asks: is that a fact or an unchecked
   assumption? (caught: "build must be narrow" — worktrees existed all along)
4. **A first-principles claim about a fast-moving external field**, made without
   checking published practice. (caught: six weeks of not knowing about
   worktrees)
5. **Sustained work in one category while the goal metric does not move** — "is
   this the work that matters?" (caught: harness grooming vs company backlog)
6. **Claim-vs-data contradiction on any observable surface.** (caught: "no
   drawable atoms" vs 30 idle atoms in the YAML)
7. **The same class of fix applied N times** — "is the class defined correctly?"
   (caught: five [ACTION NEEDED] patches guarding an undefined class)

## Design — the discipline that makes it work
- **DELIBERATELY AMNESIAC.** No memory, no accumulated context, no codebase, no
  CLAUDE.md, no access to the system's rationalisations. **A naive agent that
  accumulates context stops being naive** — it learns what is "normal here" and
  inherits the assumptions it exists to break. This is the opposite of every
  other agent in the system, and it is the whole point.
- **Inputs only:** the goal, the observable state (metrics, raw data files, the
  map as data), and the system's own CLAIMS. Not its reasoning.
- **Output: QUESTIONS, not answers.** It does not fix. It asks. Questions land
  in the digest, on the Director door, and back to the agent as findings to
  answer with evidence.
- **Strongest model** (Opus-tier): asking a good naive question is judgment, not
  simplicity.
- **Anti-capture:** it must never be tuned by the system it questions, never
  learn the house norms, never be told which questions were "unhelpful". Its
  hit-rate is a diagnostic for US, not a training signal for IT.
- **The agent must ANSWER, with evidence** — "no, that was true because X" is a
  fine answer; "that's just how it is" is not.

## What this does NOT replace
**Naivety is mechanisable. PURPOSE is not.** The naive organ can ask "should
this company optimise for enterprise value?" — but it cannot CARE about the
answer, because it has no stake in what the company is FOR. The Epoch-4 fitness
function, and every values decision, remains the director's. Not because he is
the only one who can think, but because he is the only one who WANTS.

## Why this matters beyond velocity (pitch)
"The company manufactures its own outside view" is a far stronger claim than "a
human watches it". Autonomy is only credible if the system can escape its own
frame — and this is the organ that does it. Feature it: the naive organ's
questions, and the answers given, published. The misses too.
