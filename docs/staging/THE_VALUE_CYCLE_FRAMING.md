# THE VALUE CYCLE — Epoch 2 Framing
**RATIFIED by the director 2026-07-11 (in-conversation), with three amendments incorporated: the M2 entry-gate payments audit, the Banking & Payment Rails simulator as a W-lane interface, and its Epoch-3 wall-inventory entry. This is the canonical Epoch-2 framing.**

## 1. What this epoch is

The epoch that turns a billing simulator into a company that lives in time.
One primitive delivers everything: **truth is revealed, never read** — through
interfaces, over time, provisionally, on three clocks that converge. Every
major discovery of the past week was this epoch announcing itself: the hedge
foresight bug (the future was readable), the two disagreeing revenue series
(billed vs settled clocks already diverge in our own books), the customer
ground-truth read (Q4, the wall's worst hole), the fixed population (Q6, "no
lever" for the tournament), margins too smooth-then-wild (no pricing decision
meeting no late-arriving cost truth).

## 2. The spine (one architecture, five consequences)

**Bitemporal event log** (every fact: when-it-happened + when-we-learned-it,
append-only, corrections as events) + **as-of interfaces** (every read of
market, weather, demand, customer or cost data passes through an as-of-bound
object; the future does not exist at the interface). Foundations already
begun under the bounded start. Consequences, in build order:

- **C1. Point-in-time becomes physics.** The hedge-bug class dies
  structurally (snapshot object; the PreToolUse hook remains as belt).
- **C2. Three clocks.** Billed (estimates → catch-up rebills within
  back-billing law), settled (runs on the real timetable: SF→R1→…→RF at 14
  months; MHHS compression later), banked (DD cash, failures, seasonality).
  The ledger accrues, restates, and carries unbilled income as the audited
  judgement it really is. E-lane accounts become emergent, not derived.
- **C3. The pricing organ meets late truth.** Ex-ante cost stack + explicit
  margin decision (exists, thin) now priced against *estimated* costs, with
  the ex-post margin bridge decomposing expected-vs-realised per cohort as
  true-ups land. Margin volatility becomes explained variance or a defect.
- **C4. Customers are discovered, not read.** Two-layer truth (SIM reality
  hidden; company belief assembled from onboarding + flow events, wrong
  until corrected). Population becomes a **per-run draw** (mix/skew/
  vulnerability hidden from the company) — the tournament's precondition and
  the third line on the wall: truth / belief / the draw.
- **C5. Rederived history, one clock.** Director decision stands: the whole
  2016-25 period replays through the reveal engine — real data becomes the
  harness-side answer key (discovery accuracy is the new backtest), never
  the company's feed. All published figures regenerate; the pitch refresh
  follows, never precedes, this.

## 3. What this epoch is NOT
Not the walled protocol adapters (Epoch 3 — the seam hardens after the flow
exists). Not generated futures or the tournament (Epoch 4 — but C4's draw
and C5's engine are their prerequisites, built here). Not new commercial
breadth (ToU billing structure exists; ToU tariffs wait for the preference
model). Curriculum difficulty stays the director's rack throughout (Law B).

## 4. Sequencing (four movements, evaluator-gated, cells named)

**M1 — The clock and the log** (W1→L2/L3, G→L3): bitemporal store, as-of
market/weather interface, sim loop drains events not steps, materiality
gates + lazy valuation (the affordability mechanism — compute scales with
events, not customers×ticks). *Exit test:* the hedge decision literally
cannot see past "now"; a restatement lands as an event and downstream values
version correctly.

**M2 — Three clocks through the books** (D→L3, E→L2/L3): **entry gate
first** — a payments-and-billing maturity audit of the existing 22+ modules,
graded against the D/E charters, with an Expert Hour run under a
payments-operations veteran persona (mandate lifecycle, Bacs cycle timing,
failure/representment reality, prepay), seeding the simplifications register;
each module classified COMPANY-LOGIC (behaviour) vs RAILS-PHYSICS (timing,
failure codes, cash arrival). The audit's verdict dictates M2's shape:
harden-existing vs rebuild, decided on evidence before spine rewiring. Then:
**Banking & Payment Rails simulator** as a NEW W-lane component (twin:
Bacs/bank/acquirer) built adapter-SHAPED behind an interface mirroring the
real protocol — submission windows, multi-day cycle, AUDDIS-style mandate
setup, ARUDD-style failure returns with reason codes, ADDACS amendments; cash
lands when the rails say, making the banked clock honest (rails ARE
late-arriving truth — a limb of the spine, not an adjacent system). Full
typed/versioned hardening of this seam is Epoch-3 work: add it to the wall's
adapter inventory now. Then: estimated billing
(EAC/profile) + actual-read catch-up rebilling + back-billing law; settlement
runs paying/clawing on the real timetable; DD cash engine with seasonality
and failure; ledger accrual/restatement; margin bridge v1 attributing the
lot. *Exit test:* the two revenue series reconcile BY DESIGN (they are now
named clocks with a published bridge), and a bill can be lawfully wrong then
corrected on the portal.

**M3 — Discovery and the draw** (C→L3, W2→L2/L3): customer belief layer via
onboarding/flow events; generation independently anchored from validation
(Q5's accident made deliberate); population draw per run, seeded, hidden;
run statistics become distributions over draws. *Exit test:* the company
mis-estimates a new customer's consumption, bills wrong, discovers, rebills
— end to end on surfaces; two runs with different draws produce different
books and the company cannot tell you the draw parameters.

**M4 — Rederivation and the reckoning** (C5): full history replayed through
the spine; discovery-accuracy scorecard vs the real answer key (shadow-live
philosophy applied to the past); all headline figures regenerated; margin
programme's Expert Hour re-run on the new surfaces; THEN the vision/pitch
refresh with the dual-ledger gap and honest new numbers. *Exit test:* the
director reads the rederived decade and recognises a real supplier's life.

Charters for W1/D/E/B/C/W2 (all now drafted) carry the sub-capability detail;
this framing sequences, they specify. Movements may overlap where file-scope
and the loop allow; M4 strictly last.
