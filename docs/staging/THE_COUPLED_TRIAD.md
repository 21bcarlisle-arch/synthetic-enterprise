# THE COUPLED TRIAD — depth, response, and the gap between them (P1, structural)

**Staged:** 2026-07-13 by advisor, director-decided. Disposition: **QUEUE, rank
high.** This changes what "done" MEANS on the map; apply to level definitions
and to the draw, not as a one-off build.

## The gap being closed
The map already prevents naive vertical completion (level_target is set PER
EPOCH — nothing is marching to L5 in isolation). Good. **But the W-lane (world)
and the company lanes currently advance as INDEPENDENT LADDERS.** Nothing
structurally requires that when the SIM deepens, the COMPANY must cope with the
new depth — and nothing measures how badly it copes. Every world atom could hit
its target while the supplier has never once been tested against any of it.

**Depth nobody has to cope with is scenery, not physics.**

## The triad (make it structural)
Every capability advances as a THREE-PART LOOP, not a solo climb:
1. **SIM ADDS DEPTH** — the world gains a mechanism (household budgets, life
   events, willingness-to-pay, self-rationing, DD migration).
2. **THE COMPANY MUST DISCOVER AND COPE** — through the wall only: missed
   payments, broken plans, disclosure, observable behaviour. Never by being told.
   It must be ALLOWED TO BE WRONG (that is the point).
3. **THE HARNESS MEASURES THE GAP** — between what the SIM knows (hidden truth)
   and what the company believes/does. **That gap is the score.**

## The two binding rules
- **No SIM atom reaches its "fails like reality" level (L3) until the company
  has been tested against it and the gap measured.** A world mechanism with no
  company response is incomplete by definition.
- **No company capability is complete until it has faced a world that can defeat
  it.** A collections engine never shown a household that cannot pay is
  untested, whatever its coverage.

## Belief-vs-truth GAP as a first-class metric (new, alongside levels)
Add to the map, per coupled pair: **gap** — a measured divergence between hidden
truth and company belief/action. Examples that already exist in design:
- can't-pay vs won't-pay 2x2: SIM holds the quadrant; company classifies under
  uncertainty; **harness scores classification accuracy vs hidden truth.**
- DD attribution confound: SIM holds the migration truth; the company's naive
  channel attribution SHOULD be capable of being wrong; **harness scores the
  attribution error.**
- Population draw: SIM knows the archetype mix; the company's book beliefs are
  discovered; **harness scores belief error.**
- Self-rationing: SIM knows who is heating-vs-eating; **harness scores detection
  rate and false-negative harm.**

**Report the gap in every digest and publish it on The Proof door.** A company
whose gap is FALLING is learning. A company with no gap is cheating (it read the
answer). A company with a static gap is not adapting — and that is a finding.

## Why now, not Epoch 4
This IS the tournament's engine — the arms race between a deepening world and a
coping company — and there is no reason to defer it. Run it at SMALL SCALE NOW:
every affordability atom (W2_4 through W2_10) already has its company-side twin
implied. Couple them, measure the gap, and Epoch 4 becomes a scale-up of a loop
already turning, not a new mechanism invented late.

## Consequences for the draw
- Coupled atoms are drawn as PAIRS/TRIADS where possible (world mechanism +
  company response + gap measurement), not as independent items.
- A world atom's promotion BLOCKS on its company-response test existing. Wire
  this into the level definitions, not as a reminder.

## DoD
Level definitions amended so SIM L3 requires a measured company-response gap;
gap metric defined, computed and reported per coupled pair; the affordability
cluster (W2_4-W2_10) coupled to its company-side responses and drawn as triads;
gap published in digests and on The Proof door; the two binding rules in
CLAUDE.md. **The score stops being "did we build it" and becomes "how wrong is
the company, and is it getting less wrong?"**

## DIRECTOR AMENDMENT (2026-07-13) — THE GAME IS CAT AND MOUSE, NOT CONVERGENCE
**Director, verbatim:** *"The game isn't to get to zero. It's cat and mouse. Get
ahead, catch up, get ahead again. On multiple overlapping dimensions and forcing
the trade-offs that exist in the real world."*

This CORRECTS the metric. Read it before implementing A6.

### 1. ZERO GAP IS AN ALARM, NOT A WIN
A gap of zero means one of two things, both fatal:
- the company READ THE ANSWER (epistemic wall breach — investigate immediately), or
- the WORLD STOPPED MOVING (a static SIM is not a world; a converged company in a
  frozen world is a toy).
**Wire zero-gap as a FINDING, not a success.** Sustained convergence on any
dimension triggers an investigation, not a celebration.

### 2. THE WORLD MUST RATCHET
The SIM's obligation is to keep moving: new mechanisms, new shocks, new
behaviours, new regulation. The company's obligation is to keep catching up.
**The pursuit IS the fidelity test** — a real supplier is never "done"; it is
permanently chasing a world that will not hold still. If our company converges
and stays converged, the SIM has stopped being a world.

### 3. MULTIPLE OVERLAPPING GAPS, WITH REAL TRADE-OFFS (the core of it)
There is no single gap. There are many, and **closing one OPENS another.** The
SIM must make these trade-offs REAL — i.e. it must PUNISH BOTH EXTREMES —
otherwise the trade-off is rhetorical:
- collect harder -> bad debt falls, BUT customer harm, complaints, regulatory
  exposure, redress and churn rise
- price for margin -> margin rises, BUT churn and affordability harm rise
- hedge tightly -> price risk falls, BUT collateral/working capital strangles you
  and upside is forfeited
- detect vulnerability aggressively -> catch more real cases, BUT drown in false
  positives (cost, intrusion, complaints)
- acquire hard -> growth, BUT credit quality falls and CAC rises
- estimate conservatively -> fewer catch-up shocks, BUT cash-flow drag
**Every real supplier lives on this frontier. WHERE IT CHOOSES TO SIT IS THE
STRATEGY.** The SIM's job is to make every extreme lethal in its own way.

### 4. THE SCORE (replaces "minimise the gap")
Not gap MAGNITUDE. Instead, per dimension and in combination:
- **Frontier position** — where did it choose to sit, and was that defensible?
  (a first-class strategy artefact; surface it for the director)
- **Response latency** — when the world moved, how long until it NOTICED?
- **Recovery** — after a shock, does it return to a defensible position, and how
  fast?
- **REGRET** — the formal measure: the distance between what it did and what an
  omniscient player would have done. It never reaches zero and never should.
  Falling regret over generations = learning. Regret that hits zero = a wall
  breach.

### 5. Consequence for Epoch 4
This IS the fitness function's shape: **survival on a moving frontier**, not
optimisation of a number. Build the loop this way NOW and Epoch 4 is a scale-up,
not a redesign.
