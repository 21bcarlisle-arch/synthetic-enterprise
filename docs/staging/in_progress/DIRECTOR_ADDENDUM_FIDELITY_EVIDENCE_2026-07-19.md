## The four requirements

**1. The inspection chain — four layers, navigable both directions.**
For any relationship or behaviour: (a) EVIDENCE — the underlying data and fit (e.g. a wind-vs-price scatter with the fitted relationship, its strength, and its provenance); (b) WORLD — simulated output as explorable time series, multi-variable, for the sniff test; (c) BELIEF/ACTION — what the company observed, believed at the time (point-in-time framing, never hindsight), did, and the measured belief-vs-truth gap; (d) CONSTRAINT — why the action was not the naive optimum: which constraint bound, what trade-off was made, what transversal pressure (collateral, cash, obligations) bent the decision. Navigation runs from an odd output back to its cause AND from a relationship forward to its consequences.

**2. Fidelity evidence is scored as a GRID with three measures — none of them population averages.**
- **Lift over a naive baseline**, not raw explanatory power: the obvious model (what any basic outfit has) is table stakes; value is what beats it, weighted by commercial impact. **The naive baseline must be FROZEN and independent** — the moment lift becomes a target, the baseline must be un-gameable (A5/Goodhart applies).
- **Worst-cell coverage** on the driver × (archetype × regime) grid from the campaign's scoring frame: a driver dormant on average may dominate a crisis regime or an edge cohort; a cell no driver explains is either missing physics or an untested edge — and that map of ignorance is itself a first-class view, shown honestly.
- **Ablation value of the couplings**: sever a coupling under identical seeds/populations/weather (common-random-numbers discipline — without it the measurement drowns in noise) and show what disappears. Integration that changes nothing anywhere is decorative; integration that erases the crisis when cut IS the value. This is the proof the macro↔micro and world↔supplier integration is real, not diagrammatic.
- Every relationship carries **provenance**: estimated-from-data / assumed / asserted — declared honestly; asserted ones are registered simplifications.

**3. EMIT as you build — never retrofit.**
The Epoch-2 physics work records this evidence as it goes: fitted relationships with strength and provenance, per-cell explanatory contributions, binding constraints per material decision, coupling ablation results where cheaply obtainable. A viewer over data nobody recorded is worthless; the recording requirement travels with every campaign atom's definition-of-done.

**4. The site renders it as the director's fidelity instrument.**
This is the observability the director actually wants from the site: eyeball the ranked drivers per cell, drill any relationship to its evidence, sniff-test the world, follow through to the company's beliefs/actions/gaps, and see why decisions deviated from naive optimisation. Expert Hour applies: a veteran scanning it should be able to say "yes, that's what drives it" / "you're missing X" / "why is Y in there" — all three answerable from the screen. Complexity is justified only where it beats naive somewhere that matters; that judgment must be visible, making pruning candidates (demote-with-tripwire, never delete — a dormant driver is not a dead one) visible too.
