**Type:** [STEER] — rider to `DIRECTOR_RATIFIED_PURPOSE_PITCH_V4_2026-07-19.md` (consumed moments ago). This is coherence flag 12, which the advisor missed on first staging.

## The instruction

**The website must reflect and be reoriented around the ratified purpose.** The site is the primary public expression of what this company is for, and it is currently built around the superseded framing.

## The specific coherence break

`POESYS_SITE_BRIEF.md` states the site's job as convincing a sceptical expert *"that this is a real autonomous energy company being grown in simulation toward go-live."* That is the v3 purpose. The ratified v4 purpose is materially different: **an enterprise simulator whose mission is carbon abatement through personalisation, measured as £/tCO₂e — with autonomy as the *how*, explicitly not the headline**, and with the regulatory reality that a fully autonomous licensed supplier will not be permitted stated up front.

A site built to the old brief will argue the wrong thing convincingly. This is the multiple-versions-of-truth failure mode in its most public form.

## Requirements (sequence matters; mechanism yours)

1. **Reconcile first, rebuild second.** Bring `POESYS_SITE_BRIEF.md` and `SITE_CONSTITUTION.md` into agreement with the ratified pitch before touching pages. Where they conflict, the ratified pitch wins; say plainly what changed and what is retired.
2. **The narrative spine follows the pitch's own argument** — the simulation pattern, why business never had one, what exists and where we differ, honest incompleteness, the budget as design, personalisation, reluctant homes, carbon as mission, what is not proven. The pitch is a well-ordered argument; the site should not invent a different one.
3. **The pitch's honesty disciplines become site rules, not aspirations.** Every published figure carries its claim status (observed / external benchmark / chosen / not-yet-instrumented / hypothesis / retracted), as the Note on Claims does. The pitch stakes a public claim that the company "publishes what it decided and why, and corrects itself in public" — the site is where that is either true or false.
4. **Audit, do not add.** Twenty-odd page directories currently exist. Assess each against the new purpose, propose the coherent door set, and retire or merge what no longer serves it. Propose before rebuilding.
5. **Time-sensitive honesty claims must stay accurate on the site**: no external person has yet seen this work; no household data has entered the system; no volunteer has been approached. These are load-bearing and will expire.
