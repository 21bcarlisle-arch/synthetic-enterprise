# STANDING CONSTRAINT — carbon (£/tCO₂e) is a diagnostic, never an optimisation target

**Registered 2026-07-19**, before any carbon instrumentation is built, per the advisor coherence
assessment (flag 1) on `PURPOSE_PITCH_V4.md` — the sharpest coherence risk in the whole purpose
change. The pitch states it itself (§9): *"a diagnostic, not a target… the moment a number becomes
something to optimise, it stops measuring anything, and we hold that line deliberately."*

## The constraint

**£/tCO₂e — and any metric derived from it (carbon saved, carbon spent, net carbon, the three
ledgers) — is a DIAGNOSTIC. It may be measured, reported, and inspected. It may NOT:**

1. **Enter the Epoch-4 fitness function as a maximand** (or any objective a tournament/selection
   process optimises) **without an explicit director values-call.** The Epoch-4 fitness function is
   what the company is FOR — one-way-door category 6, director-reserved. Carbon is now the stated
   *mission*, which makes this collision live and load-bearing, not hypothetical.
2. **Feed reward, selection, priority, or ranking** anywhere — not the atom draw, not the risk
   committee, not a pricing/personalisation decision loop, not a digest score. A number that steers
   the machine is a target, whatever it is labelled.
3. **Be tuned toward a benchmark.** The £273/tCO₂e (2025, 2022 prices, ±50%) government appraisal
   value is an **external-benchmark** sanity anchor (R12 plausibility band), never a goal to hit.
   A carbon reading that looks "too good" or "too bad" triggers R4 (diagnose the mechanism), never
   a tune toward the line.

## Why (the collision it prevents)

This is the same law as **LAW A** (the plan is a diagnostic, never a target) and **R12** (margin —
and any output metric — is a diagnostic, never a target; anti-goal-seek). Carbon being the *mission*
creates exactly the pressure R12 exists to resist: the strongest possible incentive to make the
headline number look good. The moment £/tCO₂e is optimised, the company learns to abate the
*measurement* rather than the carbon — and because the agent controls both the world and the
company (the epistemic-wall problem), a self-optimised carbon number would be marking its own
homework at civilisation scale. The mission is honoured by measuring carbon honestly (three ledgers,
net always reported — a claim that counts one side is not a claim), not by maximising a scalar.

## Mechanisation (how it is enforced, not just declared — MAKE_IT_STICK)

- **Now (registration):** this constraint + a `decision_log` entry; cited from the carbon-ledger
  atom's DoD when that atom is authored.
- **When carbon instrumentation is built:** the carbon ledger's own tests assert it is *read-only to
  decision surfaces* — no import of a carbon metric into the fitness function, the draw, the risk
  committee, or a pricing/personalisation reward path (a grep-guard test in the same shape as the
  raw-send-keys / bare-tmux-target guards). An unavailable/zero carbon reading must fail loud, never
  read as "great" (fail-open doctrine).
- **Director-reserved release:** only an explicit, versioned director values-call may ever make a
  carbon metric a maximand — and that call faces the director like the R13 curriculum, because the
  agent sits on both sides of the wall.
