sometimes generically called a "point-in-time view" or "snapshot" — no single universal name was
found used identically across all three traditions, but the STRUCTURE (one shared gatekeeping
abstraction, not per-function discipline) is consistent across all of them. This is a genuine,
citable case FOR eventually generalising `sim/risk_engine.py`'s pattern into a shared point-in-time
data-access seam that `company/trading/hedge_decision.py` and any future caller must route through
