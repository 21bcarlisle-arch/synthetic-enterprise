## What this is

A high-fidelity simulation of a fully autonomous UK retail energy supplier running against real
Elexon/NESO half-hourly settlement data (168,026 records, 2015–2025). The company layer cannot
see future data — all pricing, hedging, and risk decisions use only information available at the
time. Goal: detailed enough to say "that is how a real UK energy supplier works."
